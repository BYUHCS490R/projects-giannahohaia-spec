Questions

1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags? Block tags start take up the full width. Inline tags stay on the same line and take up the space of their specific content.

2. What is the margin, border, and padding? Margin is space outside the text, border is the line around it, and padding is the space between the text and the border.

3. What are units of measurement you can use in CSS? px, % , em, rem, vw and vh.

4. When would you use a figure tag? It is used for images with captions.
