How to Play:

Softball is played with two teams of nine. The pitcher throws underhand, the batter hits, and runners advance around four bases to score. Each team bats until three outs, and the most runs after seven innings wins.

Positions:

  • Pitcher
  • Catcher
  • First Base
  • Second Base
  • Third Base
  • Shortstop
  • Left Field
  • Center Field
  • Right Field
