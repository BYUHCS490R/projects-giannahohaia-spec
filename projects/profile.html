All About Jungle

Jungle is a British band formed in London in 2013. They are known for their modern fusion of soul, funk, and disco. Using a combination of live instrumentation, electronic production, and layered vocals, they create music with a unique groove and arrangement.

Most Popular Songs:

  2. Let's Go Back
  3. I've Been In Love
  4. Casio
  5. Candle Flame
  6. Keep Moving
  7. Busy Earnin'
  8. Problemz

What makes them so good?

→ Soul Revival: Their music is rooted in classic 70's soul and funk, but modenized with crisp electronic productions and layered harmonies, keeping it fresh for younger audiences. It's part of a movement that bridges generations and keeps soul alive in mainstream culture. Older listeners are reminded of Curtis Mayfield, or The Isley Brothers, while young listeners hear something new and unique.

→ Dance: While the groove of these songs is already impeccable, they are accompanied by music videos with fun, diverse dance choreographies, with renowned dancers like Will West.

→ Positivity: Their songs spread joy and good vibes with their upbeat rhythms and lyrics.
