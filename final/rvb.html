Reverb and Delay: Creating Space and Depth

Reverberation: Reflection of sound waves off surfaces in a room. It makes the listener feel like the sound exists in a real space (hall, plate, room, etc.)

In mixing, reverb is almost always applied using an aux send/return track. This ensures all elements share the same soundscape for a cohesive mix. Sweetwater: Aux Send and Return

Delay: Plays back a copy (echo) of a sound after a short, specific period of time. This gives the illusion of depth and size to a room.
