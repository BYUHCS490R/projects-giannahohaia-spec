Microphone Types

Each type is suited for different sound sources due to its internal mechanism.

  • **Dynamic:** Best for loud, close sources (snare, loud vocals).
  • **Condenser:** Best for detailed, quiet sources (acoustic guitar, studio vocals). Requires Phantom Power (+48V).
  • **Ribbon:** Warm, vintage sound; very fragile.

Microphones: Capturing the Source

The microphone is the critical first link in the audio signal chain, converting acoustic energy into electrical energy. Choosing the right mic is the most important decision an engineer makes before recording.
